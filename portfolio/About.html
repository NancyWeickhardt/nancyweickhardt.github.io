About

Past

I have always been into design. As a very young child one of my favorite toys was an easel. I loved anything visual. In college I studied studio art however I started to become more interested in graphic design and saw it was more potential for a career. I went to Syracuse to study communication design and was always interested in learning how to visually communicate messages and solve problems through design. With a focus in marketing I always understood the importance of considering the audience or the user. I began to be fascinated with how you can have great product but it won't reach your audience if you don't get into the mind of the user.
